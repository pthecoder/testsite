Precious Miracles Academy

Founded in 2018, by Andrew Creel, M.ED., initially
the agency provided early learning development to
children ages 2 to 12. The agency provides local and
remote learning with adult professional supervision.
During this time our professional practioners
discovered behavior and curriculum needs along with
staff requesting additional training. There was a need
for in home or remote one on one programming. Creel
wanted to implement training for parents to utilize long
term.

Precious Miracles conducts assessments to identify
individual needs and then develop programming to
teach what is needed. Our approach is basic and
practical, it involves the most up to date methology.
Our method is always evolving to make sure we are
meeting the needs of the community and thee
customers we serve.

We are integrative, effective as well as positive while
applying the science and knowlege to help our
customers achieve the outcomes to succeed as well build
independence to increase skills and the willingness to
try new methods.

We use evidence based practices to build on the
learners strength that will enhance the learners
confidence.
